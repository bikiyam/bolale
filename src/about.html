Our Story

Rooted in the birthplace of coffee, our journey began with a simple yet profound mission: to share the exceptional quality and unique flavors of Ethiopian coffee with the world. Our founders, with deep ties to Ethiopia's coffee-growing regions, envisioned a company that not only celebrates but also preserves the time-honored practices of coffee cultivation that have been passed down through generations.
